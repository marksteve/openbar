OpenBar

OpenBar is a versatile, open-source chat bar you can put almost anywhere.

Easy chatbars for your website

Why not create some lively discussion on your page, preferrably over drinks? Attach OpenBar as a widget to get instant chat, emojis and video support. It's really easy to setup and use! Try it out on the bottom right portion of the page.

Encrypted chat and password protection

Create your own private chatbars. Like the speakeasies of yore, create rooms only you and a chosen few are privy to. Encryption and passwords will keep away those sneaks.

Chat on any website

Install OpenBar as a bookmarklet and chat with other people who have OpenBar on any website. Getting to know people with the same internet habits as you has never been so easy!
